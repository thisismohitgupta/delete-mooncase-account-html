Account Deletion Request Information for Mooncase: Daily Savings in US Stocks

Thank you for using Mooncase: Daily Savings in US Stocks. We understand the importance of your privacy and are committed to maintaining the confidentiality and security of your personal information. This document outlines the steps you need to follow if you wish to request the deletion of your account and associated data, as well as the specifics of the data that we delete or retain.

Requesting Account Deletion

If you decide that you no longer wish to use Mooncase: Daily Savings in US Stocks and would like your account and associated usage data to be deleted, please follow these steps:

1. Send an Email Request: Compose an email to support@mooncase.io with the subject line "Account Deletion Request".

2. Provide Necessary Details: In the body of the email, please include your full name, the email address associated with your Mooncase account, and any other identifying information that may assist us in locating your account.

3. Submit Your Request: Send the email.

Upon receipt of your request, our support team will guide you through the process and inform you of any additional steps that may be required to verify your identity and finalize the deletion of your account.

Data Deletion and Retention

Data Deletion

Upon the approval of your deletion request, we will remove your personal usage data from our systems. This includes data you've provided during your use of Mooncase: Daily Savings in US Stocks, such as personal details, preferences, and usage statistics.

Data Retention

For legal purposes and to comply with regulations, we are required to maintain a record of user transactions made on the platform. This retention ensures that both you and Mooncase comply with legal obligations and regulatory requirements. The transaction data that is retained includes details of the transactions made through your account but does not include personal usage data or preferences.

Please note that the deletion of your account does not affect the retention of transaction records, which will be kept for a legally mandated period.

Questions and Concerns

Should you have any questions or concerns regarding the process of account deletion or data retention, please do not hesitate to contact us at support@mooncase.io. Our team is here to assist you and ensure that your experience with Mooncase: Daily Savings in US Stocks meets your expectations for privacy and security.

Thank you for choosing Mooncase: Daily Savings in US Stocks.
